Kings Of Ancient Egypt

Akhenaten (Amenhotep IV)

Reign: c.1353–1336 BC

He ruled during the 18th Dynasty and introduced a new religion worshipping Aten.

Nefertiti

Period: c.1353–1336 BC (Queen Consort)

She was the queen during Akhenaten’s reign and had great influence in Egypt.

Menes (Narmer)

Reign: c.3150 BC

He is believed to have united Upper and Lower Egypt.

Tutankhamun

Reign: 1332–1323 BC

He became pharaoh at a young age and his tomb was discovered intact.

Ramesses II

Reign: 1279–1213 BC

One of the most powerful pharaohs, known for great temples and battles.

Hatshepsut

Reign: 1479–1458 BC

She was one of the most successful female pharaohs in Egyptian history.

Thutmose III

Reign: 1479–1425 BC

He expanded Egypt through many military campaigns.

Khufu

Reign: c.2589–2566 BC

He built the Great Pyramid of Giza.
